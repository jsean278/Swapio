Guide

From unused gift card to cash in your account — here's exactly how Swapio works.

1

Choose Your Gift Card

Search from over 60 accepted brands — Amazon, Apple, Steam, Visa, Target, and many more. Enter your card balance ($10–$5,000) and select how you'd like to receive your cash: PayPal, Cash App, Zelle, Venmo, Bitcoin, or Bank Transfer.

2

Review Your Cash Offer

Instantly see how much cash you'll receive. Our transparent 5% service fee is shown upfront — no hidden charges, no surprises. You'll know exactly what you're getting before you submit anything.

3

Submit Your Card Details

Enter your gift card number, PIN (if applicable), and your payout account details. Provide a valid email so we can send you updates. All submissions are encrypted and handled securely.

4

Get Paid Within Hours

Our team verifies your gift card and sends your cash payout. Most swaps are completed within hours. You'll receive a unique order code (SWP-XXXXXX) to track your submission.
