Getting Paid via Venmo or Bitcoin on Swapio

4 min read

Swapio now supports Venmo and Bitcoin alongside PayPal, Cash App, Zelle, and bank transfer. Same 95% payout rate — just pick the method that fits how you actually use money.

Venmo payouts

Best for: Anyone who already sends and receives money through Venmo daily.

When you select Venmo at checkout, you'll enter the phone number linked to your Venmo account. Once your gift card is verified, we send your payout directly to that number. Most Venmo transfers complete within a few hours of verification.

Double-check your phone number before submitting — a typo means a delay while we sort it out with you.

Bitcoin payouts

Best for: Users who prefer crypto, want to hold BTC, or don't use traditional payment apps.

Select Bitcoin as your payout method and paste your wallet address. We send the USD equivalent of your offer to that address after verification. Make sure you're using the correct network and address type for your wallet — Bitcoin transactions can't be reversed if sent to the wrong place.

Does the payout amount change?

No. Whether you choose Venmo, Bitcoin, PayPal, or any other method, you still receive 95% of your verified card balance. The 5% service fee is the same across all payout options.

Which should you pick?

  • Venmo — fastest if you live on Venmo already
  • Bitcoin — best if you want crypto or prefer not to link a bank app
  • PayPal / Cash App / Zelle — still great defaults if you use them daily

Pick your method on the swap form and see your offer before you commit.
