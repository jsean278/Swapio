PayPal vs. Cash App vs. Zelle: Which Is Best?

6 min read

Swapio lets you choose how you receive your cash. All four methods — PayPal, Cash App, Zelle, and Bank Transfer — are reliable. The best one depends on your setup and how quickly you want access to your money.

PayPal

Best for: People who already use PayPal for online purchases or freelancing.

PayPal is universal and well-established. Funds land in your PayPal balance, which you can transfer to your bank or spend directly. Most Swapio PayPal payouts arrive within a few hours.

Cash App

Best for: Users who want money on a debit card quickly.

Cash App deposits go straight to your $Cashtag balance. You can cash out to your linked bank or use the Cash App card for instant spending. Popular with younger users.

Zelle

Best for: Direct bank-to-bank transfers without a middleman app.

Zelle sends money directly between bank accounts. If your bank supports Zelle, payouts feel seamless — no extra app required. Great for users who prefer keeping everything in their bank.

Bank Transfer (ACH)

Best for: Maximum simplicity — money goes straight to your checking account.

Provide your routing and account numbers, and the payout deposits directly. ACH transfers typically take a few hours to one business day depending on your bank.

Our recommendation

Choose whichever method you already use daily. There's no fee difference on Swapio's end — your offer amount stays the same regardless of payout method.
