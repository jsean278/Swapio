How to Sell a Visa Gift Card for Cash

4 min read

Visa gift cards are among the most flexible cards you can receive — and among the easiest to swap for real cash. Because they're open-loop prepaid cards, verification is straightforward and payouts move quickly on Swapio.

What you'll need

  • Your 16-digit Visa card number
  • The CVV on the back of the card (if physical) or in your email (if digital)
  • The current balance — check online or call the number on the back
  • A payout method: PayPal, Cash App, Zelle, Venmo, Bitcoin, or bank transfer

How to swap on Swapio

Search "Visa" on the homepage, enter your balance ($10–$5,000), and choose how you want to get paid. Your cash offer appears instantly — 95% of your card value with the 5% service fee shown upfront. Submit your card details and you'll receive an order code (SWP-XXXXXX) right away.

How much will you get?

A $200 Visa card pays out $190. A $75 card pays $71.25. The rate is flat across all brands — your payout method doesn't change the amount, only how fast the money arrives.

Tips for a smooth swap

  • Register your card online first if the issuer requires it — this avoids verification delays
  • Double-check the balance before submitting
  • Make sure your payout account details are correct
